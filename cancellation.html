Cancellation Policy

This policy applies to the cancellation of Hi-Res Prime subscription plans.

1. How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time by logging into your account and navigating to the 'Subscription Settings' page. The cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

2. No Partial Refunds

We do not provide partial refunds for the remainder of a billing period after cancellation. You will continue to have full access to our premium content and services until the last day of your paid subscription period.

3. Re-subscription

You may re-subscribe to a plan at any time. Your previous subscription settings and preferences will be saved, but access will be restored only upon successful re-subscription.
